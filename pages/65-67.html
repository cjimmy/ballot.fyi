Summary
Voting Yes on 67 ratifies the statewide ban on single-use plastic bags that the State has already passed. Voting No blocks the statewide ban, but city bans would remain in place.

But it gets tricky with 65. Prop 65 could negate 67 if it gets more Yes votes, even though the title doesn't sound like it would.
