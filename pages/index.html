Get educated about California's state propositions

We're tired of Donald v Hillary coverage. There are other very important issues at stake. State propositions that affect you – like weed, plastic bags, and porn – are up for vote. So it turns out your California vote is important AF.

We know you're busy, and we don't expect you to read all this in one go, so subscribe to our digest or follow on Instagram. We're here to give you everything you'll need on the California state propositions. Quickly and objectively, so you can vote smarter and harder.
